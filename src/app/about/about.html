01. About Me

With over six years in the industry, I've had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, from telecommunications and e-commerce platforms to transportation management systems. My passion lies in turning complex problems into elegant, scalable software solutions.

My expertise is in full-stack development using Angular and Python/Django. I have a strong command of modern Angular features like Standalone Components and Signals, and I leverage NgRx for robust state management. I'm also experienced in building cross-platform mobile apps with React Native.

I enjoy architecting systems from the ground up, particularly using microfrontends with Nx and microservices with Docker and AWS. This approach allows for modular, scalable, and independently deployable systems. I'm dedicated to writing clean, high-quality code and ensuring applications are accessible to all users by following WCAG standards.
